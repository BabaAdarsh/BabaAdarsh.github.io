# Teaching & Mentorship

## Teaching philosophy

I teach quantitative behavioural science the way I practice it: students learn concepts like feedback, prediction, uncertainty, Bayesian inference, and model evaluation by working with **real behavioural and physiological datasets**, not toy examples. My goal is to train students who move comfortably between behavioural theory, neuroscience, computation, and technology.
